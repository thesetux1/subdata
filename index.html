Questions

Good to know

Is this really free?

Yes. Getting matched and receiving quotes costs you nothing. Partner installers pay us a referral fee, which is how the service stays free for homeowners.

Will I get bombarded with calls?

We share your details only with the installers we match you to — up to three. You can ask any of them to stop contacting you at any time, and you're never obligated to move forward.

Do I have to buy anything?

No. The estimate is informational. Many people use it just to understand whether solar makes sense for their home before deciding anything.

How fast will I hear back?

Most homeowners are contacted within 1–2 business days, often the same day for active service areas.
